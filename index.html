About Us

Why choose Us.

2Selai & a Butter is home to some of the most-loved kotas in Soweto and beyond. We take pride in a proudly South African, township-born business that has grown from a single kitchen into a name people travel across the city for, because we never compromise on flavour, freshness, or portion size.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to elevate the classic kota while keeping it true to its Kasi roots. We want every customer, whether they grew up on kota or are tasting one for the first time, to feel welcome at our table and leave talking about the meal.

What we Do.

We carefully hollow out a quarter loaf and layer it with bold, thoughtfully composed flavour profiles, from hearty beef and rich pork preparations to fresh seafood, poultry, and plant-based creations. Each kota is crafted to satisfy a distinct craving, ensuring there's a flavour experience for every palate and dietary preference.
